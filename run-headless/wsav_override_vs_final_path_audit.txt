Question:
- In the same patched run, do the exact WSAV override cells survive to the final atlas biome recorded by inventory capture?

Patched run:
- Atlas run: 20260309-212234
- Log file: run-headless/wsav_override_plus_final_trace_run.txt

- The zero-WSAV inventory result is not a sampling artifact.

Implication:
- The previously rejected "preserve rugged savanna through final clamp" idea needs to be reconsidered, but now with exact proof:
  - the relevant bug is not speculative anymore
  - the final savanna clamp is definitely flattening genuine WSAV picks on these lattice cells
