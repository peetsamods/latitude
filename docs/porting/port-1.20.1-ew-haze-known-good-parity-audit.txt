## Known-good ownership

The known-good 1.21.1 final/hotfix path is not HUD-only.

It uses two active visual layers:

- world pass: `GlobeModClient` registers `WorldRenderEvents.AFTER_TRANSLUCENT` and calls `EwSandstormOverlayRenderer.render(...)`
- HUD pass: `InGameHudMixin` calls `EwSandstormOverlayHud.render(...)` before hotbar rendering

The older 1.21.11 under-HUD savepoint also has two owners:

- world pass: `WorldRenderEvents.BEFORE_TRANSLUCENT` calls `EwSandstormOverlayRenderer.render(...)`
- HUD pass: `InGameHudMixin` calls `EwSandstormOverlayHud.render(...)`

Fog mixins are present in the mixin manifest, but no active fog-state mutation was found in the compared EW haze path.

## Known-good draw shape

In `save/1.21.1-hotfix-final-audit`, the HUD overlay is not full-screen opaque to the bottom.

It uses:

- `HUD_SAFE_BOTTOM_PX = 86`
- `FADE_BANDS = 12`
- a vertical fade beginning around `yNorm = 0.55`
- `ctx.fill(...)` horizontal bands down to `h - HUD_SAFE_BOTTOM_PX`

The full-view storm immersion therefore appears to come from the combined model:

- the world-pass `EwSandstormOverlayRenderer` supplies the broad full-screen/under-world veil
- the HUD-pass `EwSandstormOverlayHud` supplies readable pre-hotbar haze and bottom protection
- `GlobeWarningOverlay`, compass, and title overlays render after the haze

## Current 1.20.1 difference

The current WIP diverges in two ways:

- `GlobeModClient` has the world-pass `EwSandstormOverlayRenderer.render(...)` path disabled
- `EwSandstormOverlayHud` has been tuned to `HUD_SAFE_BOTTOM_PX = 44`, `FADE_BANDS = 256`, and a lower fade start around `yNorm = 0.82`

That makes the active visual mostly HUD-pass only. Because the HUD pass is intentionally shaped to protect the lower HUD area, it cannot reproduce the known-good full-view storm immersion by itself.

## Diagnosis

The remaining 1.20.1 issue is a draw-shape / owner-parity mismatch.

The previous `GlobeModClient` world-pass disable should be superseded, not preserved as the release fix. It removed a layer that known-good versions still rely on for full-screen EW storm coverage.

`FADE_BANDS = 96` or higher can be kept as a banding smoother, but it does not fix the missing full-screen coverage on its own. The HUD overlay fade/cutoff should not be asked to do the world-pass layer's job.

## Recommended next slice

One visual-only owner-parity slice:

- restore the world-pass EW haze owner in `GlobeModClient`
- keep the HUD overlay owner in `InGameHudMixin`
- keep fog mixins untouched
- compare 1.20.1 `WorldRenderEvents.AFTER_TRANSLUCENT` versus the older `BEFORE_TRANSLUCENT` position if the restored world-pass still produces a horizon seam
- do not change warning text, compass, EW distance math, worldgen, loading, or F9/HUD Studio

Expected success condition: full-screen EW haze coverage returns while warning text, hotbar, and compass remain readable.
